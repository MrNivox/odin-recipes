Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice an asian greatly aprreciated. Its soy sauce adds an extra flavor followed by sesame oil. Wich makes it a burst of flavor upon eating. This recipes consist of different vegetables.Followed by eggs for some good protein although other protein could be added. We then have soy sauce that adds a soft litlle kick.

Ingredients

  • Sauce ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) Soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) Thai Sweet Soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon (20g) Sambal
  • 1 knob ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon (4g) toasted sesame oil
  • Ingredients
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 5-6 cups (1kg-1.2kg) Cooked Short Grain Rice 2 cups (370g) of raw rice
  • 2 chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 6oz (170g) beef flank steak, thin sliced
  • 6oz (170g) shrimp, peeled & deveined
  • 1 Fresno chili, finely chopped
  • 1 medium shallot, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 3 green onions (plus more for garnish) thinly sliced, stirred in at the end
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Pinch of white pepper
  • Pinch of MSG
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped, for finishing

Instruction

  1. In a small-medium-size bowl, mix all the ingredients. Reserve.
  2. Add butter let it melt. Then add your chicken season with salt.Sear for 2 then flip then cook for another 2 min remove and place in bowl
  3. Then add enough oil to coat the bottom of the wok; once hot, add beef and season with salt. Sear for 1 min or once you get color, flip and repeat. Remove from wok and place with the chicken.
  4. Add enough oil to coat the bottom of the wok; once hot, add shrimp, season with salt, stir fry for 2 min, add a splash of water, and stir fry for 2 minutes. Remove and place in a bowl with the rest of the proteins.
  5. To the wok add enough oil to coat the bottom, add eggs, season with salt, stir fry for 2 minutes, remove and reserve.
